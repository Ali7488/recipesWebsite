Garlic Butter Steak Pasta Recipe

Description

A rich and flavorful garlic butter steak pasta that takes about 40 minutes of actual cooking and preparation. This recipe gives you tender steak pieces, a buttery garlic sauce, and pasta that can be customized with whatever vegetables, cheese, or spices you prefer.

Ingredients:

Steak Marinade:

  • 400g steak, cut into small pieces
  • 1 TBs olive oil
  • 1 TBs soy sauce
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp chili flakes
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano

Garlic Butter Pasta Sauce:

  • 250g pasta
  • 3 TBs butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup pasta water
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Pinch of salt
  • Fresh parsley for topping

Steps:

Step 1:

Prepare the steak marinade by mixing the steak pieces with olive oil, soy sauce, paprika, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, chili flakes, and oregano. Let it sit for at least 20 minutes so the steak can absorb the flavor.

Step 2:

Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta until it is soft but still has a slight bite. Before draining the pasta, save about 1/2 cup of the pasta water for the sauce.

Step 3:

Heat a pan on medium-high heat and cook the steak pieces for 4 to 6 minutes, or until they are browned on the outside and cooked the way you like them. Remove the steak from the pan and set it aside.

Step 4:

In the same pan, lower the heat to medium and add the butter. Once it melts, add the minced garlic and cook for about 30 seconds until it smells strong but does not burn.

Step 5:

Add the heavy cream, parmesan cheese, black pepper, salt, and saved pasta water to the pan. Mix until the sauce becomes smooth and slightly thick. Add the cooked pasta and steak back into the pan, then mix everything together until the pasta is fully coated.

Step 6:

Finally, serve the pasta hot and top it with fresh parsley and extra parmesan cheese if you want. You can also add mushrooms, spinach, or chili flakes if you want more flavor. And enjoy.
