Chicken Shawarma Recipe

Description

A Lebanese-style chicken shawarma that takes about 45 minutes of actual cooking and preparation. This recipe gives you a base of garlicky, flavorful chicken with plenty of room for customization, so you can add whatever toppings and sauces you like.

Ingredients:

Chicken Marinade:

  • 2 TBs yogurt
  • 2.5 TBs vegetable oil (corn oil is best)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp shawarma spices
  • 2 tsp vegetable broth
  • 1 TBs VINEGAR
  • 1 TBs LEMON
  • 1/2 tsp chicken spice
  • 1/2 tsp Powder Onion
  • 1/4 tsp Powder Garlic
  • 1/4 tsp Coriander
  • 1/4 tsp Chili
  • 1/4 tsp Nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp Cumin
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric

Garlic Yogurt Sauce Mix:

  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 TBs corn starch
  • 500g of yogurt
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 1 TBs vinegar
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

Step 1:

Prepare the chicken marinade and mix then leave the chicken in it for at least 3hrs (Leaving it overnight is best).

Step 2:

Meanwhile, start the garlic yogurt mix by adding a cup of water and 2 TBs of corn starch to a pot, and mix without heat at first.

Step 3:

Once you mix it for a bit, put the stove on medium heat and mix until the mixture becomes more viscous (gooey). Add the mixture to a blender with your yogurt, garlic cloves, cooking oil, vinegar, and a pinch of salt (pink salt tastes a bit better but normal table salt works well). Blend until the mixture is thick but not solid and refridgerate until you are ready to cook and assemble the shawarma.

Step 4:

Put the chicken on a pan and set the stove to high for 5 minutes, then medium for 15 minutes (if it is not fully cooked put it back on high until it is done).

Step 5:

Once everything is done, it is time for assembly. Take your bread of choice (tortilla works well) and if you want the classic shawarma look bend the bottom inwards, then the left and right sides, and place the sauce (about a teaspoon works fine) on the bottom, then chicken on top of it, and whatever toppings you want (pickles and fries if you want a more classic taste).

Step 6:

Finally, roll the tortilla up starting from the bottom, and put it back in the pan for a few minutes on both sides to toast a bit (optional but recommended). And enjoy.
